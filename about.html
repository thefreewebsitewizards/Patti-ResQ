Guidance for Life’s In-Between Seasons

I’m Patti L. Curtin, founder of Journey in Twilight, a place for education, reflection, and practical support during life’s in-between seasons. It was created for the moments that don’t have quick answers, the ones that require patience, understanding, and compassionate guidance instead.

One of the most meaningful expressions of that work is ResQ a Dog: Save a Life, Training the Human, which comes first. I created ResQ after years of working alongside rescue dogs and the humans who love them. Again and again, I saw adoptions struggle, not because of lack of love and not because people didn’t care, but because they weren’t prepared for what the dog had been through.

ResQ slows the process down and shifts the focus where it belongs: the human. I help people honestly assess their lifestyle, expectations, and capacity for care while learning to recognize a dog’s needs beyond first impressions. This approach leads to safer adoptions, stronger bonds, and better outcomes for both ends of the leash.

My work is rooted in patience, trust-building, and the belief that when education is delivered with compassion, rescue dogs are given their best chance at a stable, loving home, and humans are given the confidence to succeed.

Because when the human is prepared, the dog finally gets a real chance.
